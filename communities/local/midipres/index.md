MiDiPres was set up in 2020 by Rachel MacGregor (University of Warwick) and Laura Peaurt (University of Nottingham). It is an informal peer support community for digital preservation practitioners in the Midlands region that aims to provide support, shared experience and a sounding board for issues faced by those working in the region.


It is aimed to support practitioners at all levels of experience from beginner to advanced and many of our members tackle digital preservation challenges as only one part of a much broader role. 


Meetings take place online and generally take place 3-4 times per year.


Meetings usually last for two hours and are based on a co created agenda often including tool demonstrations and shared experiences.


We welcome new members at any time. Please do get in touch!
